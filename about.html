About Truth or Dare Generator

Truth or Dare Generator is a free online tool made to do one thing well: generate random truth or dare prompts — clean, fun questions and dares for parties, teens, kids, and couples.

We built it because the best web tools are the simple ones — no sign-up, no downloads, no clutter. Open the page, get your answer, get on with your day. It works on phones, tablets, and desktops, and it will always be free to use.
